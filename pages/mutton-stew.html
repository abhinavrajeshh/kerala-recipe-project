Mutton Stew recipe




About Kerala's Mutton stew: Succulent chunks of mutton simmered in delicate southern flavors of coconut milk, curry leaves, cinnamon, etc. Try it with steamed rice and enjoy the wholesome meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500 gms cubed mutton (boiled with 10 gm ginger)
  • 100 gms onion, sliced
  • 5 gms green chillies (slit)
  • 10 gms ginger strips
  • 1 tsp pepper corns
  • 10 Curry pattas
  • 1 coconut, grated
  • 100 gms oil
  • 150 gms cubed potatoes
  • 50 gms diced carrot
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom and cloves

Steps :

  1. Boil mutton along with ginger. Set aside.
  2. Boil potatoes and carrots. Set aside.
  3. Extract coconut milk twice.
  4. Heat oil and saute sabut garam masala, onion, green chillies, ginger, curry patta, peppercorns.
  5. Add boiled mutton, potatoes and carrot along with second extract of coconut milk and cook. Add salt to taste.
  6. Stir first extract of coconut milk just before serving.
